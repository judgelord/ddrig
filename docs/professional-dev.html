Professional development plan

No longer than 1 page, single-spaced 500 words

Proposals must include a brief professional development plan describing steps the research scholar plans to take to advance his or her research career. This should include an explanation of how the research scholar envisions the dissertation project fitting into an expected long-term research agenda as well as an indication of a professional development opportunity (or opportunities) at which the research could be presented and improved. In addition, the professional development plan may identify further training that will enhance the research scholarâ€™s long-term research agenda, plans and opportunities for creating or joining professional networks, and plans and opportunities for sharing research in public spaces.
